About Shiksha Sankalp


What is Shiksha Sankalp?

Shiksha Sankalp is an endeavour to educate even the poorest child in India. We help to bring about the change using innovative 'performance based cash incentives'. It is aimed at facilitating the development of our human resource for sustained econmic growth of the country between now and 2050.

Legal Status

We are registered in India and the USA.

Legal status in India

In India, Shiksha Sankalp Foundation (SSF) has been registered as a Trust. It is being registered with Income Tax Department under section 12A and would also apply for tax-exemption for donors under section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Further, it is also applying for FCRA clearance for receiving donor funds from abroad.

Legal status in USA

In the USA, a separate organization called Shiksha Sankalp Incorporated (SSI) has been formed and tax exempt status under section 501 C(3) obtained. SSI’s mandate is to promote school education in all developing countries. SSI’s mandate is to promote school education in all developing countries. However, initially it is focused on India where it coordinates efforts with SSF. The SSF and the SSI are independent organizations and their cooperation is governed by a legal agreement for sharing of resources (including intellectual property), transfer of funds and implementation of programs. Each organization is free to partner with other NGOs for obtaining funds as well as for implementation of programs.
