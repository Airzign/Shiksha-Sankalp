What is Shiksha Sankalp ?


Shiksha Sankalp is a resolve to ensure that every Indian child achieves age-appropriate level of academic knowledge and skills. The project is geared towards developing human capital for the demographic opportunity for accelerated economic growth of India between now and 2050. Shiksha Sankalp is a Non-Governmental Organization – a Society (being) formed under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. We are currently in the process of registering the Society with Government of National Capital Territory of Delhi. The Society is being formed with the specific objective of fostering rapid expansion of school education and reduction of poverty in India by incentivizing academic performance of students from economically weak families. Organisational structure – governing council and panel of independent experts
